Cristiano Ronaldo

About Ronaldo

Born: February 5, 1985

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Al Nassr and the Portugal national team. Born in 1985, he is widely regarded as one of the greatest football players of all time, known for his incredible goal-scoring ability, athleticism, and dedication to the sport.

Important Events

  1. 1985 - Born in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal.
  2. 1997 - Joined Sporting CP youth academy.
  3. 2003 - Signed with Manchester United.
  4. 2009 - Transferred to Real Madrid.
  5. 2018 - Joined Juventus.
  6. 2021 - Returned to Manchester United.
  7. 2023 - Signed with Al Nassr.

Achievements

  • Five-time Ballon d'Or winner
  • UEFA Champions League titles
  • International success with Portugal
  • The greatest goal scorer of all time
